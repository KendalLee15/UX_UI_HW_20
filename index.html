North Carolina Dental Society Foundation

The NC Dental Society Foundation helps dentists and patients through coaching and communication. They facilitate treatment and prevention education for adults and children in need through free-clinic settings and support local community clinics. The goal for this redesign is to ensure that the NC Dental Society Foundation website is user-centered, meeting the needs of patients, motivating dentists to participate, and providing valuable community information. By implementing the research findings, the website will be better positioned to achieve its goals and serve its target audience effectively.
